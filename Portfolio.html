Israfil Memmedov

The national hero of Azerbaijan

The first Azerbaijani hero in Soviet Union


Here is the timeline of Israfil

  • 1919, March 16 - Born in Shamkir, Azerbaijan.
  • 1939 - He went to military service at the age of 20. Israfil served in Pustinka, Novqorod.
  • 1941 - At war against Germans he and his companions fought bravely. Near the end of fight there only left Israfil and 8 companions. He protected Pustinka village till the end.
  • 1941, December 11 - He was awarded the title of Hero of the Soviet Union by the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR.
  • 1941, December 15 - He was awarded the Order of Lenin and Hero of USSR "Golden Star".
  • 1941, May - He was injured 1st time at war and paused his service while restablishing his health.
  • 1943, May - He was injured 2nd time and again paused military service.
  • 1946, May 1 - After the 3rd injury he closed his eyes eternally. His body is buried in Ganja.

"I am 22 years old. Now I see my meaning of life as destroying more fascists. While I'm alive I will fight against fascism with this goal."

-- Israfil Memmedov, speech after being awarded
